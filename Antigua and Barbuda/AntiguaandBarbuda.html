Weather

Between the tiny islands of Antigua and Barbuda, temperatures range from 70 degrees Fahrenheit to 80 degrees Fahrenheit year round. It is considered one of the sunniest places of the Eastern Caribbean Islands, but has low humidity year-round.

Beaches

Barbuda's coastline is edged with smooth pink and white sand beaches. These beaches stretch as far as ten miles without interuption. Just beneath the shallow water, the island is protected by barrier reefs. Antigua has 365 beaches, one for each day of the year! Most of these beaches are resting in the protected waters of the island's Caribbean side.
